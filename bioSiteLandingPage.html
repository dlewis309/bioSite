Where it all started

Originally from Peoria Illinois, Asa lewis Jr. is truly the definition of a started from the bottom, rose to the top success story. After graduating from college, Asa knew immediately that his passion resided in youth development and coaching. He would go on to pursue a degree in education from which he will actually graduate this year. It wasn’t always sunshine and happy thoughts for Mr. Lewis though. Peoria Illinois is a dangerous and destructive city for most urban youth, and becoming the coach of many of these children, he was able to see this up close and personal. Without even trying, Asa was able to become more than just a coach to the children on his teams, he became a mentor and friend. His work with the children was applauded by the school and the community, leading him to begin work inside the school as well, providing guidance to troubled youth. This alone would be cause for celebration, but future tragedy would be the catalyst for his greatest accomplishment. On Feb 12th, 2017, his younger brother Marques was shot and killed while trying to prevent the shooting of a stranger at a valentine’s day party. While the grief was all but unbearable, Asa quickly saw this as a wakeup call to try and to lead change within his community. He went on to create a non-profit called Make Your Marq in honor of his brother. He has received rewards and accolades for his service.
